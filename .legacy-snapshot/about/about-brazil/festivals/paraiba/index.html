1. Festivals Overview

Paraíba is shaped by São João traditions, coastal culture, and strong folk heritage.

2. Festival Calendar (By Month)

  • Jan–Feb: Carnaval de João Pessoa
  • Mar–Apr: Semana Santa
  • May: Festa do Trabalhador
  • Jun: São João de Campina Grande
  • Jul: Festa Junina
  • Aug–Sep: Festa das Neves, Semana da Pátria
  • Oct–Nov: Festival de Música, Festival Literário
  • Dec: Natal Iluminado, Réveillon

3. Top Annual Festivals (Flagship Events)

  • Festa das Neves
  • Festival de Música
  • Festa do Forró
