1. Festivals Overview

Piauí’s festivals showcase inland traditions, local cuisine, and community celebrations.

2. Festival Calendar (By Month)

  • Jan–Feb: Carnaval de Teresina
  • Mar–Apr: Semana Santa
  • May: Festa do Trabalhador
  • Jun: Festa Junina
  • Jul: Festival de Inverno de Pedro II
  • Aug–Sep: Festival Literário, Semana da Pátria
  • Oct–Nov: Festival Afro-Piauiense, Festival de Dança
  • Dec: Natal Iluminado, Réveillon
