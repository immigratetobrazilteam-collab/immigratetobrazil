How Immigration to Brazil Works

A practical, step-by-step path from eligibility to settlement.

Step-by-step process

1. Eligibility assessment

We evaluate your goals, visa options, and constraints.

2. Pathway selection

We choose the most reliable visa route and alternatives.

3. Document preparation

We compile, translate, and legalize required documents.

4. Consular filing

We guide your submission and appointment process.

5. Travel planning

We align entry timelines with legal requirements.

6. Federal Police registration

We prepare you for in-country registration.

7. CPF and onboarding

We help with CPF, banking, housing, and healthcare.

8. Long-term compliance

We handle renewals and status updates.

Timeline guidance

Timelines vary by visa type, documentation readiness, and appointment availability. We provide a realistic schedule during your assessment.

  • Pre-application planning and document collection
  • Legalization and translation window
  • Consular or in-country submission
  • Post-arrival registration and CPF
