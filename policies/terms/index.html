Apply to Brazil: step-by-step overview

These are the core steps described on the application guide.

  1. Choose your visa type and confirm eligibility.
  2. Collect required documents and certified copies.
  3. Translate and apostille documents where required.
  4. Find your Brazilian consulate and schedule an appointment.
  5. Submit your application and pay fees.
  6. Track approval timelines and respond to requests.
  7. Travel to Brazil within your visa window.
  8. Register with Federal Police and obtain your residency card.
